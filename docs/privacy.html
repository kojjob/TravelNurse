Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2024

1. Introduction

TravelNurse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile details you provide during registration.
  • Financial Data: Expense records, income information, mileage logs, and tax-related data you enter into the app.
  • Documents: Files you upload to the Document Vault, including licenses, contracts, and receipts.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for mileage tracking (only when you enable this feature).

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including features accessed and time spent.
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes to help us improve stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the TravelNurse application
  • Track your expenses, mileage, and tax-related information
  • Generate reports and tax summaries
  • Send you notifications about tax deadlines and important updates
  • Improve our app based on usage patterns
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Process subscription payments through Apple's App Store

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your financial data, documents, and personal information are stored locally on your device. We do not store your sensitive financial data on our servers.

On-Device AI Processing: Our AI features (Tax Assistant, Smart Categorization) process data entirely on your device using Apple's Natural Language framework. No financial data is sent to external AI services.

Encryption: All locally stored data is encrypted using iOS's built-in data protection features.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Apple for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., exporting data to your accountant).

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can access all your data within the app at any time.
  • Export: You can export your data in standard formats (JSON, CSV).
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app settings.
  • Location: You can enable or disable GPS mileage tracking at any time.
  • Notifications: You can manage notification preferences in the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
